Trip Essentials

⛽ Fuel map — every working pump
  • Leh — 2 pumps (main bazaar IOC + Choglamsar)
  • Karu — 35 km from Leh on Manali highway
  • Diskit — the ONLY Nubra pump; hand-operated at times, can run dry
  • Tangtse — 34 km before Pangong; last pump before Changthang
  • Nyoma — ~75 km before Hanle
  • Hanle — no pump; homestays sell petrol in black (reserve ahead)
  • Nothing on Shyok road, Wari La, Turtuk road, Mahe→Karzok (Tso Moriri) spur, or beyond Hanle

High altitude cuts mileage 30–40%. Carry a 5–10 L jerry can from Leh for the Nubra→Pangong→Hanle→Umling La stretch.

📋 Permits (2026, Indian nationals)

No separate ILP — pay the Environment & Development Fee online at lahdclehpermit.in (~₹590 for the trip, valid 21 days). Portal unlocks only after 48 h in Leh. Tick Nubra + Pangong + Hanle + Umling La circuits. Carry 8–10 photocopies for the South Pullu, Tangtse, Mahe and Loma checkposts — no network to show digital copies.

Foreigners: PAP via a Leh agent; generally not permitted to Umling La.

🌧️ Late-July reality check
  • Peak monsoon influence: the Shyok road flooded badly in July 2025 (roads under 2–3 m of mud in places). Decide the Nubra→Pangong route on the morning of Day 4, not before.
  • Khardung La timing: uphill 9 AM–1 PM, downhill 1–5 PM. Leave Leh by 7–8 AM.
  • All water crossings (Shyok nalas, Chang La streams, Photi La): cross before 11 AM–noon — glacial melt doubles them by afternoon.
  • Fallback hierarchy if Shyok is shut: back-to-Leh + Chang La (paved) > Wari La (rough — Option 3 riders only).
🏔️ Umling La checklist
  • Confirm army clearance in Leh AND at Loma checkpost on Day 5
  • Depart Hanle 5–6 AM; summit by 11; descend by 2 PM
  • Full tank + 5 L spare; no services beyond Hanle
  • Portable oxygen can + warm windproof layers (sub-zero windchill even in July)
  • Max 10–15 min at the summit (~5,798 m)
  • Final climb: sealed tarmac. Photi La approach: steep loose gravel + streams — slow, feet-ready
  • Bail triggers: overnight rain, AMS symptoms, or road closed → observatory + stargazing instead
🎒 Pack list highlights
  • Diamox (consult doctor) + portable oxygen + basic med kit
  • 5–10 L jerry can, bungee straps, puncture kit, spare tube/clutch cable
  • Cash — ATMs only in Leh, Diskit, Nyoma (often dry)
  • BSNL/Jio postpaid SIM (only networks that work in Changthang; patchy)
  • Red-light torch for Hanle Dark Sky nights
  • Waterproof riding gear — it's monsoon-adjacent season
